About

This site is a small portfolio of some of the music I've enjoyed over the years!

The amount of screenshots, notes, and saved playlists across my old and current devices is pretty ridiculous. Once in a while I'll really want to listen to a certain track, but I can hardly audiate it's structure or lyrics, let alone the title of the song. Recently I learned about the Personal Knowledge Management system. This small-scale 'second-brain' system is a great way to practice my code and keep track of my current favorites.

I have a really big problem staying on track and keeping up with my coursework. Coming into this class was an absolute breath of fresh air. In the middle of a very routine semester, we had our first meeting as a class. The content was a little confusing at first, but I quickly picked up on how to efficiently copy+paste (jk). No, getting to spend some time with these front end languages gave me some confidence in myself i wasn't feeling at the time. I realized how far I'd come in a years time when it came to understanding concepts and problem solving. I feel like i soaped up so much information from lectures, texts, YouTube videos, and articles and I know I've still hardly scratched the surface. I have a lot to build on still, but it's been a fun experience.
